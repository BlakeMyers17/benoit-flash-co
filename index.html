About

If you’re looking for delicate watercolor butterflies or a Pinterest-friendly infinity symbol, keep walking. I draw flash that punches you in the face with a cigarette still hanging from its lip. My style is pure American Traditional — bold lines, pissed-off panthers, weeping daggers, and skulls that actually look like they’ve seen some shit. I grew up with grease under my nails, sarcasm in my blood, and a deep respect for the kind of art that looks good on a bar napkin or your forearm. I don’t do “customs” unless it’s a case of beer and you’re cool to hang. Flash is king. If you like it, slap it on you. If not, there’s always clip art.
